Overview

Computers have many intricate parts that work together seamlessly. Each component plays a vital role in keeping the system running—from processing data and storing information to displaying images and connecting to networks. Together, these parts form a complex yet efficient system that allows computers to perform millions of tasks in seconds, making modern life faster, easier, and more connected.

Main Components

  • CPU (Central Processing Unit)
  • RAM (Memory)
  • Output Devices (Monitor, Speakers, etc.)
  • Motherboard (Main Circuit Board)
  • Power Supply Unit (PSU)

ComponentDescriptionExample

Quick Tips

  1. RAM helps your computer multitask. More RAM = smoother performance.
  2. Without the power supply, nothing runs.
  3. The motherboard connects all components within the computer.
