Overview

Computers have many intricate parts that work together so seamlessly behind the scenes that most people rarely stop to think about how much is happening inside. Each component plays a vital role in keeping the system running smoothly—from processing data and storing information to displaying images and connecting to networks. Together, these parts form a complex yet efficient system that allows computers to perform millions of tasks in just seconds, making modern life faster, easier, and more connected.

Main Components

  • CPU
  • Ram (Memory)
  • Output Devices (Monitor,Speakers,etc)
  • Motherboard (Main Circut board)

Ram (Memory)Temporary storage that holds data currently being used or processed.16GB DDR5 RAM

Quick Tips

  1. Ram helps your computer multitask. More Ram = Smoother performance
  2. Without the powersupply nothing runs
  3. Motherboard connects everything within the computer

Traits of the First Computer

  1. Large Size: Early Computers were enourmes, often taking up an entire room.
  2. Very Expensive: Cost millions of dollars to bild and maintain.
  3. Slow Processing Speed: Could only perform a few thousand calculations per second.

History

  • First general purpose electronic computer in the USA was the ENIAC invinted around 1945-1946
  • Another was the Colossus from the UK which was built to break german codes suring WWII (1943-1944)
  • First commercially available computer was the UNIVAC 1 invinted in 1951 (only for business and government use)
